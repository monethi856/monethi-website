AfriSkii Winter Fest

Winter Wonderland in the Heart of Africa

Piched high in the majestic maluti mountains of Lesotho, AfriSki Winter Fest 2026 invites to experience winter like never before. Against a backdrop of snow-capped peaks and crisp alpine air, this premier mountain resort tranforms into a vibrant hub of music, culture and adventure. From sunrise ski sessions to sunset gatherings around crackling bonfires, everyday brings new opportunities to connect, celebrate and embrace the majesic of the season.

Beyond the slopes, immerse yourself in a carefully curated line up of live performances, goument mountain dining and night pulse until the early hours. Whether you're a seasoned skier, a music lover or simply seeking an extraordinary escape, AfriSki winter Fest delivers an unforgattable blend of thrill and tranquility.
