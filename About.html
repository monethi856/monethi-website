History of Winterfest at Afriski

The Winterfest event is a winter festival hostes annually at AfriSki Mountain Resort, located in Maluti Mountains of Lesotho. The resort itseelf opened in 2002 and became the main skii destination in Southern Africa.
Winterfest was later introduced as a snow-themed music and adventure festival to celebrate the winter ski season and attract visitors to the resort. The festival has been running since the mid 2010 and quickly grew into one of the most unique winter festivals in Southern Africa.
The event usually takes place towards the end of the ski season and lasts for several days.
It combines:

  • Skiing and snowboarding competitions
  • Live music performance from local and regional artists
  • Adventure activities such as tubing, bum-boarding and quard biking
  • Entertainment and nightlife on the mountain

The Significance of AfriSki Winterfest

The Afriski Winterfest promotes Lesotho as a winter tourism destination by highliting unique attractions such as snow, skiing and other winter sports. The event also contributes to the local economy by generating revenue for the tourism industry through services like accommodation facilities, restaurents,activities and transport. Additionally, it attracts visitors from different places, encouraging cultural exchange and strengtthening Lesotho's image as an adventure tourism destination.

Target Audience

  • Young adults and adventure seekers interested in winter sports such as skiing and snow boarding
  • Domestic tourists who want winter entertainment and mountain experience
  • International tourists who travel to experince snow activities in Africa
  • Music and festival lovers attracted by live performance and entertainment
  • Adventure and sports tourists interested in outdoor mountain activities
